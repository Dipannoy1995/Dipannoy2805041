Project information

  • Category: Networking
  • Objective: Cache Replacement Policy
  • Simulator: NDN

Named Data Networking(NDN) was my undergrad research topic. I have proposed a new cache replacement policy for the content store of NDN.

NDN woks with content rather than IP. It's also called as Content Centric Networking. The contents reside in the content store of NDN nodes. But the nodes don't have unlimited memory. So, eviction of content to give place to new content is often required. I have basically worked in this part. Selecting appropriate content for eviction was the target of my algorithm. The policy I have proposed is named as "Hybrid Policy". It performs better than default NDN policy- LRU, Priority Fifo. This work has been submitted to NSys 2021.
